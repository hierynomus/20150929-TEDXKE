What is the record deepest depth for freediving (apnea diving)?

A) 214m

B) 145m

A) 214m

The record depth of 214m was set by Herbert Nitsch in 2007 in the "No Limit" category. 145m is the depth set by William Winram in 2013 in the "Variable Weight" category.

Do the Dutch waters contain poisonous fish?

A) Yes

B) No

A) Yes

The Greater Weever (Grote Pieterman) is a poisonous fish, and its sting is potentially lethal

Which of these two causes more deaths per year?

A) Great White

B) Box Jellyfish

B) Box Jellyfish

The box jellyfish kills on average 20-40 people in the Philippines alone, and estimates are 100 worldwide. In 2014 out of 130 shark attacks, only 3 were fatal.

Can you dive when you have a cold?

A) Yes

B) No

A) Yes

You can, but it might have some nasty side-effects such as mask-squeeze as you might be unable to equalize

Are you allowed to dive without a diving-knife?

A) Yes

B) No

A) Yes

You can, but having it with you just might save your life.

Can you get dehydrated under water?

A) Yes

B) No

A) Yes

Due to the increased pressure and lower temperature of water you will get immersion diuresis. This can cause dehydration with increased chance of decompression illness

What is more important to do underwater?

A) Holding your breath underwater

B) Blowing out your breath while ascending

B) Blowing out while ascending

Failing to blow out can cause pulmonary barotrauma leading to a pneumothorax. Holding your breath is not advisable as it increases the chance of nitrogen narcosis

A regular diving bottle is filled with?

A) Compressed oxygen

B) Compressed air

B) Compressed air

Pure (100%) oxygen is lethal below 8m (1.6 ppO2).

What is the maximum depth you can dive on regular air?

A) 40m

B) 60m

B) 60m

40m is the depth limit for most organizations (and insurances) for "sport" diving. At 60m regular air becomes toxic. Any deeper you should dive with a hypoxic mixture.

Can small fish kill you?

A) Yes

B) No

A) Yes

The Blue Ringed Octopus is 12-20cm and the Fugu (pufferfish) 23cm are among the most lethal animals

How long do you need to wait after diving before flying?

A) 24 hours

B) 18 hours

B) 18 hours

The PADI guidelines state minimum 18 hours for repetitive diving.
